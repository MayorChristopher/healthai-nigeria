## JUDGE Q&A PREPARATION

**Q: Is this medically validated?**
A: "No. This is a 48-hour prototype. Before any real deployment, we'd need a medical advisory board to review every aspect."

**Q: How do you handle liability?**
A: "Currently, we don't. A production version would need comprehensive disclaimers, terms of service, and professional liability insurance."

**Q: What about hospital partnerships?**
A: "We don't have any yet. The hospital list is public information. Real partnerships would be our first priority post-hackathon."

**Q: How will you make money?**
A: "We haven't decided. Options are: stay open-source, freemium model, or B2B hospital partnerships. We're focused on proving value first."

**Q: Why should we believe you can build this?**
A: "We built a working prototype in 48 hours. We're honest about limitations. We're coachable. Give us mentorship and we'll build it right."
